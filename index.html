Social Determinants of Health in Toronto (SDH TO)

The purpose of this web application, Social Determinants of Health in Toronto (SDH TO), is to educate and inform people of the inequality within Toronto. This is intended to be accomplished through the analysis of spatial data relating to various social determinants of health (e.g., education level, visible minority, Aboriginal identity, income level) and the distribution of COVID-19 cases.

The Relationship Between Social Factors and Crime Rates in Toronto: An Analysis

This section reports our analysis of property crimes in Toronto, Canada, and its relationship with household income, unemployment rates and education level. Data were obtained from the Toronto Police Service Portal and the City of Toronto

GNSS driver for Ublox (ZED-f9P)

This project is modified based on Kumar Robotics ublox Gnss driver, specifically tailored for configuring u-blox 9 devices using protocol version 27.1. This enhanced driver not only facilitates u-blox configuration but also offers the capability to display GNSS raw data, TIM2 messages, and fix status messages.
